Welcome to The Spore Trail

The Spore Trail is my digital journey into the world of photography, hiking, and exploration. It is my personal HUB, where I share my adventures, insights and creative outputs.

Why This Site?

I am using micro.pub as my provider. I'm transition over to a website I can fully control and take part in the IndieWeb movement to take back our internet. This website is my online identity, a HUB where anything I post gets POSSE'd out to my various other social media accounts where people can find me. The real beauty of it all though is that a congregation of all my posts, thoughts, opinions and even replies on all social media platforms can be found on this website. So you can be sure never to miss out on anything should you wish to follow my content directly, at the source. What's even better is you can choose to reply to me directly from this website if you have an account with Micro.blog, Mastodon, or Bluesky, making it also a community HUB.

What You'll Find Here

  • Hiking Photos: Capturing the beauty of trails and nature.
  • Audio Recordings: Sounds from the trails, ambient nature, and more.
  • Short Posts: Quick updates, thoughts, and reflections.
  • Articles: In-depth stories and insights from my journeys.
  • Video Links: Links to video content showcasing my adventures.

For more about me, visit the About section.
